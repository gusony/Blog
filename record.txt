***SSH quick connect:*** 
1.in ~/.ssh/config
2.vim ~/.ssh/config
    Host short-name
    HostName domain-name
    User username
    Port 22
3.in terminal :SSH short-name
4.connected

***SSH keygen:****
1.ssh-keygen -t rsa (generate rsa key to ~/.ssh/id_rsa.pub)
2.1:ssh-copy-id -i .ssh/id_rsa.pub hostname # 推薦V
(2.2 optional):scp id_rsa.pub server_hostname:~/.ssh # (擇一)缺點, 機器沒有.ssh目錄時，會有問題
3:finish

***tar***
1.tar -jcv -f filename.tar.bz2 要被壓縮的檔案或目錄名稱
2.tar -jxv -f filename.tar.bz2 -C 欲解壓縮的目錄


***vim .vimrc theme***
1:create ~/.vim/colors dir
2:vim ~/.vimrc 
    syntax enable
    colorscheme monokai
3:finish
